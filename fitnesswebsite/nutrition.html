Key Nutrition Tips

Protein Priority

Consume 1.6-2.2g of protein per kg of body weight daily to support muscle growth

Hydration

Drink 3-4 liters of water daily to boost metabolism and reduce water retention

Metabolism Boost

Include metabolism-boosting foods: green tea, chili peppers, coffee, and ginger

Avoid Processed Foods

Eliminate processed sugars and refined carbs to reduce bloating and fat storage

Timing Matters

Consume complex carbs earlier in the day and focus on protein/veggies for dinner

Healthy Fats

Include healthy fats from avocados, nuts, and olive oil for hormone balance

Note: Combine this nutrition plan with targeted strength training and cardio for best results
